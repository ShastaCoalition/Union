Common Questions

Things people wonder about unions.

"I can negotiate for myself â€” I don't need a union."

You can â€” but alone, you're one person. A union is collective bargaining power: your employer must legally negotiate with the group. "Unions are just for factory workers or blue-collar jobs."
+
Not even close. Today, unions represent teachers, nurses, journalists, tech workers, baristas, grad students, actors, and flight attendants. +
